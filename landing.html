Why Parents Choose Tiny Tots 💛

Everything your child needs to build a strong learning foundation — in one fun app.

🎮

Learning Through Play

Every lesson is a game. Kids earn stars, unlock levels, collect stickers and keep daily streaks — making them WANT to come back every day.

🎯

Built for Their Age

Content automatically adjusts to your child's age. A 3-year-old sees colors and animals. A 7-year-old gets spelling, math and phonics.

🔊

Real Animal Sounds

Not robot noises — actual animal sounds. Kids hear a real dog bark, lion roar, and monkey call. Learning that sticks!

👩‍🏫

Interactive Teacher

When a child gets something wrong, a friendly teacher appears and visually walks them through the answer step by step — no frustration!

🦊

Custom Avatar Buddy

Kids pick their own animal buddy, choose colors and add accessories. Their buddy cheers them on and reacts to their answers!

🔒

100% Safe & Ad-Free

No ads. No in-app purchases. No scary content. Just pure, safe, educational fun designed specifically for young children.

📱

Works on Any Device

Phone, tablet, or computer — Tiny Tots works everywhere. No download needed. Just open the browser and start learning!

🎨

Free Time Too!

After learning, kids can color pictures, draw freely, and explore. Balance between structured learning and creative play.

🔥

Daily Streaks & Rewards

Kids build learning habits with daily streak counters, star collections, and level-up celebrations that keep them motivated!
